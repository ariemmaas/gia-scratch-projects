🎯 How to play :

🖱️ 1. Use your mouse (browser)

👆 2. Use your finger (touchscreen)

💢 3. Chase the balls

⭐ Everytime you catch the balls, they'll explode!

🐞 CHASE THE DRAGON BALLS! 🔫



💥 CLAW GAME 💥

🎯 How to play :

🖱️ 1. Use your mouse (browser)

👆 2. Use your finger (touchscreen)

💢 3. Click or tap above the penguin doll you want to pick

⭐ Once you click or tap, release your finger to make the claw steady!

🐞 GET ALL THE PENGUIN DOLLS! 🔫



💥 THROWING CHEESY PUFFS 💥

🎯 How to play :

🖱️ 1. Click on the Cheesy Puffs to pick

👆 2. Click on the green button to throw

💢 3. If you aim right, you'll hit some apples

⭐ Once you take down all the apples from the tree, you win!

🐞 TAKE DOWN ALL THE APPLES! 🔫
